## Known Limitations

- Damodaran score uses ROIC + PEG + leverage as a valuation proxy.
  A full reverse-DCF is deferred (see `pipeline.py` `_score_damodaran` docstring).
- Macro Radar works from stored `CompanyAnalysis` data only; raw filing
  text is not persisted, so cross-company text analysis is not available.
